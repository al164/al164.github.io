Projects

Blue Exchange

In a team of four, we designed an app to help students find classes taught by other students. Through interviews, we found that a centralized platform would be helpful for students who wanted to learn something new or get tutoring help, but didn't know where to look. For this project, all team members contributed equally to design and user intereviews.

FOMO Finder

In a team of four, we created a website that would help students find local and on-campus events. Through interviews, we found that students often had trouble finding events, especially since not all events are publicized on the same platforms. Thus, we created a website that would allow students to post events and see other local events. For this project, I contributed most to design and front-end coding.

Web Design and Emotions: A Literature Review

Through extensive research, I wrote a detailed literature review of how different factors of web design can affect viewers’ emotions

Solitaire

In a team of three, we coded the back-end of a simple solitaire game using Java.
